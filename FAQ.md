Q1. I did not apply. Can I still contribute?

Yes. No application is required to participate in this repository.

Q2. Will contributors who didn’t apply be evaluated?

All public contributions are visible as part of open collaboration.
Contributors are not formally evaluated, labeled, or ranked in this repository.

Q3. Is this a hiring or recruitment process?

No. This repository functions as an open thinking and collaboration space.
Any collaboration opportunities, if they arise, are separate and may occur later.

Q4. Can students participate and use this for learning?

Yes. Participation for learning, exploration, and skill development is encouraged.

Q5. Can international contributors participate?

Yes. Participation is open regardless of geography, institution, or background.

Q6. What kind of contributions are expected?

Contributions may include:
- Thoughtful reasoning and analysis
- Critical perspectives and constructive critique
- Conceptual models or synthesis of ideas
- Exploration of trade-offs and open-ended problems
There are no assignments and no “correct answers”.

Q7. Will contributors be contacted directly?

In rare cases, contributors demonstrating sustained and relevant public work may be contacted if it aligns with ongoing internal exploration.
Most participation does not result in direct outreach.
