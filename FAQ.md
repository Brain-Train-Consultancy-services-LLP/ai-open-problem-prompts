Q1. I did not apply. Can I still contribute?

Yes. Application is not required.

Q2. Will contributors who didn’t apply be evaluated?

All public contributions are visible. We do not label or rank contributors.

Q3. Is this a hiring process?

No. This is an open thinking space. Collaboration opportunities, if any, emerge later.

Q4. Can students use this for learning?

Yes. That is encouraged.

Q5. Can international contributors participate?

Yes. Geography and background are irrelevant here.

Q6. What kind of contributions are expected?

Thoughtful reasoning, critique, synthesis, and exploration. There are no “correct answers”.

Q7. Will I be contacted?

Only in rare cases where sustained work aligns with ongoing internal exploration.
