# AI Maintenance Problem

## Problem Context
AI models degrade over time due to changing data and environments.

## Core Question
How can AI systems detect and correct their own performance decay?

## Why This Matters
Unmaintained AI becomes unreliable and risky.

## Exploration Directions
- Drift detection
- Continuous evaluation
- Automated retraining
- Monitoring systems
