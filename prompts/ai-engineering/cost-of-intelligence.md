# Cost of Intelligence

## Problem Context
Advanced AI systems are expensive to build and run.

## Core Question
How can low-cost AI solutions be built for resource-constrained environments?

## Why This Matters
Access to AI should not be limited to wealthy organizations.

## Exploration Directions
- Efficient models
- Hardware-aware design
- Edge and offline AI
- Cost-performance trade-offs

Ideas for affordability and scalability welcome.
