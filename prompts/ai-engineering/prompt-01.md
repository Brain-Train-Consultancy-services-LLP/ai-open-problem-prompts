### Prompt Title

**Minimum Team Size:** 5  
**Suggested Roles:**
- AI / ML Engineer
- Backend / Systems Engineer
- Documentation / Product Thinker

---

### Problem Context

Describe the problem in simple, real-world language.
Avoid technical jargon where possible.

---

### The Challenge

What makes this problem difficult?
What constraints exist?
What decisions must be made?

---

### What Teams Should Explore

Teams may explore:
- Design choices
- Ethical considerations
- Trade-offs and risks
- Human involvement
- Governance or scalability concerns

---

### Expected Outcome

There is no fixed output.

Teams should produce:
- Clear documentation
- Reasoned decisions
- Evidence of collaboration

---

### Evaluation Focus

- Depth of understanding
- Quality of reasoning
- Team collaboration
- Clarity of explanation

For evaluation criteria and transparency, please refer to the Evaluation Framework repository.
