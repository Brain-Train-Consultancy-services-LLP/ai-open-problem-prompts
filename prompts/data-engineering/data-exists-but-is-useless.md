# Data Exists but Is Useless

## Problem Context
Organizations collect massive amounts of data, but most of it is never used.

## Core Question
Why does having more data not automatically lead to better decisions?

## Why This Matters
Wasted data means wasted money, effort, and opportunity.

## Exploration Directions
- Data quality vs data quantity
- Poor data structure
- Missing context
- Human misunderstanding of data

Business and data perspectives are welcome.
