# Unheard Voices

## Problem Context
Large sections of society are invisible in most datasets.

## Core Question
How can AI represent people who are under-documented or ignored?

## Why This Matters
Decisions based on incomplete data exclude real human experiences.

## Exploration Directions
- Alternative data sources
- Community-driven data collection
- Ethical representation
- Bias awareness

## Collaboration Notes
Open to social, technical, and policy perspectives.
