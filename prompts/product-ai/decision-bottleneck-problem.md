# Decision Bottleneck Problem

## Problem Context
Critical decisions depend on a few experienced individuals.
This creates delays, risk, and scaling limitations.

## Core Question
How can AI reduce dependency on individual expertise without replacing humans?

## Why This Matters
Organizations slow down when knowledge is concentrated in few people.

## Exploration Directions
- Knowledge capture systems
- Decision support tools
- Human-in-the-loop AI models
- Trust and accountability design
