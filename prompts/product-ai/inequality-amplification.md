# Inequality Amplification

## Problem Context
Technology often benefits those who are already privileged.

## Core Question
How can AI be designed to reduce inequality instead of increasing it?

## Why This Matters
Unchecked systems widen social and economic gaps.

## Exploration Directions
- Fairness metrics
- Inclusive design
- Policy-aware AI
- Redistribution mechanisms

## Collaboration Notes
Equity-first thinking is encouraged.
