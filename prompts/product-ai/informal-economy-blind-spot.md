# Informal Economy Blind Spot

## Problem Context
Large parts of the economy operate outside formal data systems.

## Core Question
How can AI model and support informal economic activity?

## Why This Matters
Millions of livelihoods remain invisible to policy and finance.

## Exploration Directions
- Alternative economic signals
- Community-level data
- Trust-based systems
- Ethical modeling

## Collaboration Notes
Economic, social, and policy perspectives are welcome.
