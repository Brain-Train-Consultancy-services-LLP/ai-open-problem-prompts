# Invisible Inefficiencies

## Problem Context
Many industries lose money without knowing where or why.
Inefficiencies remain hidden due to scale, complexity, or human blind spots.

## Core Question
How can AI identify inefficiencies that humans routinely overlook?

## Why This Matters
Hidden inefficiencies silently reduce profit, productivity, and quality.
Small improvements at scale can create large impact.

## Exploration Directions
- Pattern detection in operational data
- Anomaly detection in workflows
- Human and AI collaboration for insights
- Explainable outputs for decision-makers

This prompt is open for discussion, critique, and real-world examples.
