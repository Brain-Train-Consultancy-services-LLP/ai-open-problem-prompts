# Workforce Skill Mismatch

## Problem Context
Jobs exist, but workers often lack required skills.

## Core Question
How can AI help reskill rather than replace workers?

## Why This Matters
Reskilling preserves jobs and economic stability.

## Exploration Directions
- Skill mapping systems
- Personalized learning paths
- AI-assisted training
- Ethical workforce transition
